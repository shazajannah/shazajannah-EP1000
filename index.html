Digital Fabrication & Prototyping is an elective module offered from Singapore Polytechnic to all Singapore Polytechnic students. It offers a wide range of teaching elements that introduces the students to the fundamentals and aspects of said topic. With more hands-on exercises and mostly self-directed learning with pointers from the learning resources, it equips students with the basics of design and technology, but with a more in-depth theory/practical.

This site is created to log the exercises and processes of the creator of this website for the current and ongoing project of creating her final product which is to be created by the end of the EP1000 Digital Fabrication & Prototyping module. This includes sketches, screenshots, pictures and more.
